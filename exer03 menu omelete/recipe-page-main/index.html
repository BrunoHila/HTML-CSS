An easy and quick disc, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl the eggs with a pinch of salt and Pepper until they arer

  2. Heat the pan: Place a nan-stick frying pan over medium haet and add buter or oil.
  3. Cook the omelette: Once the butterr is melted and bubbling, pour in the eggs. tile the
    pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface
  4. Add fillings(optional): When the eggs begin to set at the edges but are still slightly
    runny in the middle, sprinkle your chosen fillings ove one half of the omelette.
  5. Fold and serve: As the omelette continues to cook, carefully lift on edge and fold it

  6. Enjoy: Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed.
